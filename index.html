Ikhwan Abdillah Affan

I am a Graphic Designer from Cianjur specializing in visual branding, UI design, and storytelling through impactful visuals. My journey began in middle school with Photoshop, where I learned photo editing and layout, and continued in high school as I strengthened my skills in Adobe Illustrator for logo creation and brand identity. Over time, I expanded my creative range by exploring 3D modeling with Blender to enhance visual depth and flexibility in my projects. With a strong foundation in visual communication and a commitment to producing high-quality work, I strive to deliver design solutions that are consistent, engaging, and elevate the character of every brand I work with.
